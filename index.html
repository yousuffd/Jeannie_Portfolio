About

The leader behind the data.

I'm a Director-level Finance BI and Sales Strategy executive with over two decades of experience at the intersection of data, technology, and business leadership. My career has been defined by one consistent outcome: turning complex, fragmented information into the clear metrics that executive teams act on.

At Mandiant (part of Google Cloud, formerly FireEye), I led a 12-year tenure directing Finance Business Intelligence through every phase of the company's evolution from growth, through a major divestiture coordinated with the DOJ, to a full integration into Google Cloud. I built and led BI/IT teams of remote and offshore resources, governed enterprise-wide data initiatives, and delivered financial analytics for SEC and Wall Street reporting.

"I don't just deliver reports. I build the analytical infrastructure that makes an organisation smarter, and keeps it that way through every transition."

Outside of pure BI, I bring strategic depth to sales operations: contributing to Go-to-Market planning, compensation design, sales performance analysis, and renewal forecasting. I understand how data connects to commercial outcomes and build systems that keep that connection strong.
